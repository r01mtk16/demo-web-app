My Taste in Music

I have an impecable taste for country, ragae, and gospel music. These kinds of music inspire me and uplift my spirit. Country music for example, makes me reflect about life and the plans for my future and the future of my children. Ragae music on the one hand, encourages me to move on in life against all odds. Gospel music, helps me reflect on my relationship with God, and the need to show love to this dying world. The world needs of love now, more than ever.
